    Adidas was started in 1920 by the German shoe designer, Adi Dassler who worked alongside his brother, Rudolf to build a successful sports footwear company.     Adidas have a long history of association with Olympic athletes. Today, Adidas sponsor various sporting teams, from the New Zealand All Blacks rugby team to the English cricket team.

    Adidas use the technology they develop for the worlds most successful sports teams to design high quality sportswear, equipment and footwear for consumers.
